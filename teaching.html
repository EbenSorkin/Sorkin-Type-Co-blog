Teaching



Eben Sorkin teaching at Crafting Type in Edmonton Alberta, Canada in 2012. Photo: Rob & Lauren

Focus

Lectures are geared to students and professionals engaged in:

  • • Publication design
  • • Graphic design
  • • Information Design
  • • Wayfinding and sign making
  • • Brand and identity
  • • Marketing
  • • Documentation
  • • Games
  • • Architects
  • • Artists

    • Subjects include:

  • • Typography
  • • The Science of reading and what it offers type designers
  • • Beginning Type design
  • • Sources of personality in type design
  • • Typographic and use issues to be aware of and test for when designing type
  • • Methods of testing type
  • • Optimizing type for use in specific media

Begining in 2009, Eben Sorkin began teaching type design and typography. Eben has taught in the USA, Canada, the Czech Repulic, Poland and Iceland.

This year he will teach with Crafting Type. Crafting Type workshops will be available in a wide variety of cities worldwide.
