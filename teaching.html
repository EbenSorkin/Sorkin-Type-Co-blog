Teaching

Begining in 2009 Eben Sorkin began teaching internationally on the subjects of type design and typography. Eben Has taught in Canada, the Czech Repulic, Poland and Iceland. This year he will continue to teach with CraftingType. Crafting Type workshops will be available in a wide variety of cities worldwide. To see where Crafting type will be and to request that they visit your city go to the crafting type web site.

Subjects

The subjects Eben Teaches about include beginning Type design, the science of reading and what it offers type designers, the sources of personality in type design, typographic or use issues to be aware of when designing type, methods of testing type, optimizing type for use in specific media and typography.

Students

Students who will benefit from Eben's lectures include those involved in or studying graphic design, brand and identity, wayfinding and sign making, marketers, architects, and artists.
