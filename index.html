Purpose Driven
Type Design

A well-designed font project doesn't just look good. It also pays significant dividends on the investment. Our clients take advantage of this value and benefit from a business centered, custom typeface design. They range from multinationals such as Google and Facebook, startups and small business. Sorkin Type develops fonts in a wide range of styles and scripts for a wide range of media.

About

Eben Sorkin incorporated Sorkin Type in 2011. We have made over 60 open source fonts for Google. These fonts can be used and downloadedfor free at Google web fonts.In late 2013 commecial version of some of these fonts will become available.These commecial versions will offer design refinements, a larger number of styles and OpenType features.

Teaching

Begining in 2009 Eben Sorkin began teaching internationally on the subjects of type design and typography. This year he will continue to teach with CraftingType. Crafting Type workshops will be available in a wide variety of cities worldwide. Be in touch to request that CraftingType come to your city.
