About

Sorkin Type was formed in 2011. So far have made over 60 open source fonts for Google. These can be seen and are free to use at Google web fonts. We expect to begin offering commecial web and print version of these fonts in a larger number of styles and with Open type features during the next year.

Our approach

We make type because we love doing it. We know that if you need type made you have different reasons. You need the type to do a job and to pay off as an investment. Perhaps you need type to help make a brand stronger or to make it easier to read for an aging audience, or for one of 100 other possible reasons. For us the important thing is to understand your needs and to make you aware of your options so you can make the most of the type you have made.

Will you be using this type in a variety of environments? Sorkin Type can optimise your type design to work across a range of printing conditions and on a wide variety of computer systems and screens.

Fixing things

Sometimes you don't need something compltely new. Instead you just need your font to work a little better or to work in a new media environment or a new size or in a new format.We can help with these problems too.

Pushing boundries

Sorkin Type has been pround to work as a tester of early release version of a new Autohinter called TrueTypeAutohinter or TTFA. We belive this tool will become an invaluable tool for type foundries both large and small in the future.
