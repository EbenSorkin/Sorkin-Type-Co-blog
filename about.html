Our approach

We love making functional type. A fuctional type is tuned to a specific purpose the media it will be used in and has the right feeling. Type is a great asset in for enhancing or developing a brand, improving customer experiences, and for improving readability. For us the important thing is to understand your needs and priorities and to make a type that serves you well.

Of course sometimes you don't need something new. Often we can adapt and improve existing type as well.

Pushing boundries

Sorkin Type has been pround to work as a tester of a new Autohinter called True Type Autohint or TTFA. We belive this tool will become an invaluable tool for type foundries both large and small in the future.

Eben Sorkin

Eben Sorkin incorporated Sorkin Type in 2011. We have made over 60 open source fonts for Google. These fonts are free to use and downloaded at Google Fonts.In late 2013 commercial versions of some of these fonts will become available. These commecial versions will offer design refinements, a larger number of styles to choose from and OpenType features. Eben Sorkin is a member of ATypI and serves on its board.
